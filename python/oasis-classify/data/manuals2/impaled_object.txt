Category: Impaled Object

- Call 911 immediately.
- Do not remove the object — it may be preventing fatal hemorrhage.
- Build up bulky padding (folded cloths) around both sides to stabilize it.
- Secure padding with bandages; do not press down on the object.
- Control bleeding by pressing surrounding tissue, not the object itself.
- Do not remove the object regardless of its size or location.
- Do not press directly on the object.
