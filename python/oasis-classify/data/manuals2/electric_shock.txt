Category: Electric Shock / Electrocution

- Do not touch until power source is confirmed off — you will also be electrocuted.
- Call 911 immediately; turn off power at main switch or circuit breaker.
- If power cannot be cut, use dry non-conductive object to push source away.
- Once safe to approach, check for responsiveness and breathing.
- If not breathing, begin CPR — cardiac arrest is common after electric shock.
- Do not touch the person until the power source is off.
- Do not use wet or metal objects to move a live electrical source.
