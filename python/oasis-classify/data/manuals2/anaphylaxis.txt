Category: Anaphylaxis / Severe Allergic Reaction

- Call 911 immediately — anaphylaxis is life-threatening.
- Administer epinephrine auto-injector into the outer thigh without delay.
- Lay person flat and elevate legs unless breathing is difficult.
- If breathing is difficult, allow person to sit upright for comfort.
- Give second epinephrine dose after 5-15 minutes if symptoms persist.
- Do not delay epinephrine to see if symptoms improve on their own.
- Do not use antihistamines as a substitute for epinephrine.
