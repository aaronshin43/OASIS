Category: Dental Injury

- Handle knocked-out tooth by crown only — do not touch the root.
- Rinse gently with milk or saline if dirty — do not scrub.
- Reinsert tooth into socket immediately if person is conscious; bite down on cloth.
- If unable to reinsert, store in milk or inside person's cheek.
- See dentist or emergency room within 30-60 minutes — survival drops after 1 hour.
- Do not scrub the tooth root or let it dry out.
- Do not store the tooth in plain tap water — it damages root cells.
