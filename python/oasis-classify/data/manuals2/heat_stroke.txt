Category: Heat Stroke / Heat Exhaustion

- Call 911 for heat stroke (hot dry skin, confusion, no sweating, high temperature).
- Move person immediately to a cool shaded or air-conditioned area.
- Remove excess clothing.
- Cool rapidly: apply ice packs to neck, armpits, and groin; fan while misting with cool water.
- If fully conscious and able to swallow, give cool water to drink.
- Do not delay cooling while waiting for emergency services — start immediately.
- Do not give fluids to an unconscious person or one who cannot swallow.
