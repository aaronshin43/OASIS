Category: Suicidal Crisis

- Stay calm; do not leave the person alone.
- Ask directly: "Are you thinking about suicide?" — asking does not increase risk.
- Listen without judgment; do not minimize their pain.
- Remove any means (medications, weapons) from reach if safe to do so.
- Call 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) together, or 911 if in immediate danger.
- Do not leave the person alone if there is immediate danger.
- Do not promise to keep their suicidal thoughts secret.
