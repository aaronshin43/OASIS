Category: Severe Bleeding / Hemorrhage Control

- Call 911 immediately.
- Apply direct pressure to wound using clean cloth or bare hands.
- Press hard — maintain continuous pressure for at least 10 minutes.
- If blood soaks through, add more material on top without removing first layer.
- If limb bleeding fails direct pressure, apply tourniquet 5-7 cm above wound.
- Do not remove an embedded object from the wound — press around it instead.
- Do not remove the first cloth layer to inspect — it disrupts clotting.
