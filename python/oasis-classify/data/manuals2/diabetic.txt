Category: Diabetic Emergency

- If conscious and able to swallow, give 15-20 g fast-acting sugar immediately.
- Use glucose tablets, juice, or sugary drink; have person sit and rest.
- Repeat sugar if no improvement after 15 minutes.
- After improvement, give complex carbohydrate snack to maintain blood sugar.
- Call 911 if unconscious, cannot swallow, or does not improve.
- Do not give food or drink to an unconscious person.
- Do not give insulin without a blood glucose reading.
