Category: Bear Encounter / Bear Attack

- Do not run — running triggers bear's chase instinct.
- Grizzly attack: fall face-down, cover neck with clasped hands, spread legs. Play dead.
- Stay completely still and silent until grizzly leaves.
- Black bear attack: do not play dead — fight back hard, strike nose and eyes.
- Use bear spray at 8-10 meters as bear charges, aimed at face.
- Do not run from any bear — you cannot outrun one.
- Do not play dead with a black bear — fight back instead.
