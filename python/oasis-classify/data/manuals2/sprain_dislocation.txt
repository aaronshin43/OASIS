Category: Sprain / Dislocation

- Stop activity immediately; rest the injured joint.
- Apply ice wrapped in cloth for 20 minutes to reduce swelling.
- Apply a compression bandage firmly but not too tightly.
- Elevate the injured limb above heart level.
- For dislocation: immobilize in position found; do not attempt to reduce it.
- Do not attempt to pop a dislocated joint back without medical training.
- Do not apply heat in the first 24-48 hours — it increases swelling.
