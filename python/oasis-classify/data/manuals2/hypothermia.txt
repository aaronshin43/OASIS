Category: Hypothermia

- Call 911 if person is severely hypothermic, unresponsive, or not breathing.
- Move to a warm dry location; remove wet clothing and replace with dry layers.
- Cover including head with blankets; rewarm core first.
- Apply warm packs wrapped in cloth to neck, armpits, and groin.
- If unresponsive and not breathing, begin CPR — a cold heart can still be resuscitated.
- Do not rub limbs — cold blood from extremities can trigger cardiac arrest.
- Do not give alcohol — it causes further heat loss.
