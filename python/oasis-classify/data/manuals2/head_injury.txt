Category: Head Injury / Concussion

- Call 911 if unconscious, confused, vomiting repeatedly, unequal pupils, or skull deformity.
- Keep person still; minimize movement of head and neck.
- Control scalp bleeding with gentle direct pressure using a clean cloth.
- If unconscious but breathing, place in recovery position.
- If not breathing, begin CPR immediately.
- Do not leave the person alone for at least several hours after injury.
- Do not give alcohol, sedatives, or pain medication that can mask symptoms.
