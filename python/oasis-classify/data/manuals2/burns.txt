Category: Burns

- Remove person from heat source; stop the burning immediately.
- Cool burn with cool running water for 10-20 minutes.
- Remove jewelry and loose clothing near burn before swelling occurs.
- Cover cooled burn with clean non-fluffy material (cling film or clean cloth).
- Call 911 for burns larger than palm, deep burns, or burns on face or hands.
- Do not use ice, butter, toothpaste, or oils — they trap heat and increase infection.
- Do not pop burn blisters — they protect the wound.
