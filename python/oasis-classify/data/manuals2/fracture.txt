Category: Fracture / Broken Bone

- Call 911 for open fractures, spine/pelvis/femur breaks, or signs of shock.
- Immobilize the limb as found using a padded rigid splint on both sides.
- Secure splint above and below the fracture; check pulse/sensation beyond it.
- Cover open fractures loosely with a clean dressing.
- Apply ice wrapped in cloth to reduce swelling.
- Do not attempt to straighten or push bone back into the wound.
- Do not apply splint so tightly it cuts off circulation.
