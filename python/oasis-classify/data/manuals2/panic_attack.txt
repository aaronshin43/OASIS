Category: Panic Attack

- Stay calm; speak slowly and reassuringly.
- Remind them: this feeling is not dangerous and will pass.
- Coach slow breathing: inhale 4 counts, hold 4, exhale 4, hold 4. Repeat.
- If breathing too hard, try grounding: name 5 things you see, 4 touch, 3 hear.
- If chest pain, arm pain, or symptoms last over 20 minutes, call 911.
- Do not just say calm down -- guide them through breathing instead.
- Do not restrain or crowd the person.
