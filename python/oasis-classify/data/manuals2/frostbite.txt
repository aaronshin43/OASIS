Category: Frostbite

- Move person to a warm environment immediately.
- Remove wet gloves, socks, or footwear from affected areas gently.
- Do not rewarm if refreezing is possible — refreezing causes worse damage.
- Rewarm by immersing in warm water 37-40 degrees C for 20-30 minutes.
- Cover rewarmed area with dry dressings; pad between frostbitten fingers or toes.
- Do not rub or massage frostbitten tissue — ice crystals cause further damage.
- Do not use fire, stove, or heating pad — uneven heat burns numb tissue.
