Category: CPR / Cardiac Arrest

- Confirm unresponsiveness; call 911 immediately.
- Tilt head back, lift chin, check breathing for no more than 10 seconds.
- Place heel of hand on center of chest; compress at least 5 cm deep.
- Compress at 100-120 per minute; give 30 compressions then 2 rescue breaths.
- Use AED as soon as available — turn on and follow all voice prompts.
- Do not stop compressions repeatedly to check for a pulse.
- Do not compress too slowly or shallowly — depth and rate are critical for blood flow.
