Category: Drowning / Near-Drowning

- Ensure your safety — throw rope or extend branch from shore; call 911.
- Once on shore, check responsiveness.
- If not breathing, begin rescue breathing: give 5 initial rescue breaths.
- Use head tilt chin lift to open airway, then start CPR at 30:2 ratio.
- If breathing, place in recovery position on side; keep person warm.
- Do not attempt to drain water from lungs before CPR — begin breathing support immediately.
- Do not assume person is safe after rescue — secondary drowning can occur hours later.
