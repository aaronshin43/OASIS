Category: Fainting / Syncope

- If person feels faint, have them lie down or sit with head between knees.
- If fainted, lay on back and elevate legs 20-30 cm to improve brain blood flow.
- Loosen tight clothing at neck, chest, and waist.
- Check breathing — if not breathing normally, begin CPR.
- Call 911 if unconscious more than 1-2 minutes or person has chest pain.
- Do not give food or drink until person is fully conscious and alert.
- Do not allow person to stand immediately after fainting.
