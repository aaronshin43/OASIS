Category: Stroke

- Use FAST: Face drooping, Arm weakness, Speech slurred, Time to call 911.
- Call 911 immediately; note exact time symptoms started — treatment depends on it.
- Keep person calm, still, and comfortable.
- If unconscious and breathing, place in recovery position on their side.
- If not breathing, begin CPR.
- Do not give aspirin unless emergency services instruct it — stroke may be hemorrhagic.
- Do not give food or water — swallowing is often impaired after stroke.
