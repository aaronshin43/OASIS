Category: Shock

- Call 911 immediately; treat underlying cause (apply direct pressure to severe bleeding).
- Lay person flat on their back.
- Elevate legs 20-30 cm unless head, spine, chest, or lower limb injury prevents it.
- Keep person warm with a blanket; loosen tight clothing at neck and chest.
- Monitor breathing and pulse; be prepared to start CPR.
- Do not raise the legs if head, chest, or spinal injury is suspected.
- Do not give food or drink by mouth.
