Category: Wildfire Escape

- Check wind direction — fire travels fastest uphill and downwind.
- Move perpendicular to the fire's path to reach an escape route.
- Protect airway: wet a cloth and hold over nose and mouth to filter smoke.
- Stay low in heavy smoke — air is cooler and cleaner near the ground.
- If escape is cut off, find a ditch or cleared area with minimal vegetation; lie face-down, hands protecting face.
- Do not run uphill toward a wildfire — fire moves uphill faster than you can run.
- Do not ignore wind shifts — fire direction can change and cut off escape routes suddenly.
