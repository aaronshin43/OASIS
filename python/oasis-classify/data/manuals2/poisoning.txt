Category: Poisoning / Toxic Ingestion

- Call 911 or Poison Control immediately.
- Identify what was ingested, how much, and when — report to emergency services.
- Follow specific instructions from Poison Control if person is conscious.
- If unconscious and breathing, place in recovery position.
- For skin or eye contact, flush with large amounts of clean water for at least 20 minutes.
- Do not induce vomiting unless Poison Control specifically instructs you to.
- Do not give food, milk, or water to neutralize the poison without professional guidance.
