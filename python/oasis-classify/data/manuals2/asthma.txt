Category: Asthma Attack

- Sit person upright, leaning slightly forward with hands on knees.
- Help use rescue inhaler (blue, short-acting): shake, place in mouth, press.
- Give 1 puff every minute up to 10 puffs while waiting for help.
- Call 911 if no improvement after 10 puffs or breathing worsens.
- Begin CPR if person becomes unresponsive and stops breathing.
- Do not lay the person flat during an asthma attack.
- Do not substitute a long-acting brown or purple inhaler for the rescue inhaler.
