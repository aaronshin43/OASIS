Category: Wild Animal Attack

- Do not run — running triggers predator chase response.
- Stand tall, raise arms, open jacket wide; group with others.
- Speak loudly and firmly; shout to appear threatening.
- Mountain lion attack: fight back immediately — strike nose, eyes, throat.
- After attack: control bleeding and call 911 for medical help.
- Do not run from or turn your back on a mountain lion, wolf, or coyote.
- Do not play dead with a mountain lion — fight back aggressively.
