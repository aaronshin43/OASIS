Category: Acute Stress Reaction

- Remove person from ongoing danger before addressing psychological symptoms.
- Speak slowly using their name; use short, calm sentences.
- Offer warmth, water, a blanket — physical comfort anchors awareness.
- Use grounding if dissociated: ask what they can see or feel right now.
- Call 911 if severely unresponsive or unable to care for themselves.
- Do not pressure them to describe or relive the traumatic event.
- Do not leave someone who is severely dissociated or unresponsive alone.
