Category: Seizure / Epilepsy

- Call 911 if seizure lasts more than 5 minutes or person does not regain consciousness.
- Clear hard objects away; place something soft under the person's head.
- Time the seizure from the start.
- Gently turn person onto their side to keep airway clear.
- After seizure stops, check breathing; begin CPR if not breathing.
- Do not put anything in the person's mouth — they cannot swallow their tongue.
- Do not hold the person down or restrain them during a seizure.
