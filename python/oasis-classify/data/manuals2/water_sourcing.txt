Category: Water Sourcing / Dehydration

- Recognize dehydration: dark urine, dizziness, dry mouth, rapid heart rate.
- Moving water (streams) is safer; purify all wild water before drinking.
- Boil: rolling boil 1 min; above 2000 m altitude, boil 3 minutes.
- Chemical: iodine or chlorine tablets per instructions; wait 30 min.
- Severe dehydration: give fluids slowly; call 911 if confused or unconscious.
- Do not drink seawater -- it accelerates dehydration and damages organs.
- Do not drink urine -- it concentrates waste and worsens dehydration.
