Category: Eye Injury

- For chemical splash: flush eye with large amounts of clean water for 20 minutes.
- Tilt head so affected eye is lower to prevent contaminating the other eye.
- For foreign body: do not rub — blink rapidly or flush with clean water.
- If object is embedded or eye is impaled, cover gently with clean cup or cone.
- Cover both eyes with clean dry dressing to reduce eye movement; seek care.
- Do not rub the eye when a foreign body is present.
- Do not attempt to remove an embedded or impaled object from the eye.
