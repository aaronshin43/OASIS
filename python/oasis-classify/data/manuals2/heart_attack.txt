Category: Heart Attack

- Call 911 immediately.
- Help person sit in a comfortable semi-reclined position; loosen tight clothing.
- Give 300 mg aspirin to chew slowly if not allergic and conscious.
- Keep person calm and still — movement increases heart workload.
- If person loses consciousness and stops breathing, begin CPR immediately.
- Do not let the person walk around or exert themselves.
- Do not delay calling 911 hoping symptoms will pass.
