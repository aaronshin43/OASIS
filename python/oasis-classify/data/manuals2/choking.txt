Category: Choking / Airway Obstruction

- If cannot speak, cough, or breathe, act immediately.
- Give 5 firm back blows between shoulder blades.
- If back blows fail: fist just below ribcage above navel, thrust inward and upward.
- Alternate 5 back blows with 5 abdominal thrusts until expelled.
- If unconscious, lower to floor, begin CPR, check airway each breath.
- Do not perform a blind finger sweep -- remove object only if clearly visible.
- Do not give abdominal thrusts to infants -- use back blows and chest thrusts.
