Category: Spinal Injury

- Call 911 immediately. Tell person not to move.
- Stabilize head: place both hands gently on either side, hold in position found.
- Do not attempt to straighten the neck or spine.
- Maintain stabilization until EMS takes over.
- If unconscious and not breathing, perform jaw thrust without tilting head; begin CPR.
- Do not move the person unless there is immediate life threat.
- Do not tilt the head or attempt to correct any deformity.
