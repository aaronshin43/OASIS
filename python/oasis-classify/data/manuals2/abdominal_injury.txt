Category: Abdominal Injury

- Call 911 immediately.
- Lay person on back, knees slightly bent to relax abdominal muscles.
- If organs are protruding, cover with clean cloth moistened with clean water.
- Cover moist dressing loosely with plastic wrap to retain moisture.
- Keep person warm to prevent shock.
- Do not push protruding organs back inside.
- Do not give food, drink, or oral medication before surgery.
