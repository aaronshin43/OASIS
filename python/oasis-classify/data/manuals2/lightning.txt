Category: Lightning Strike

- Confirm area is safe — lightning can strike the same location again.
- Call 911 immediately. It is safe to touch the victim — no residual charge.
- Check for responsiveness and breathing.
- If no pulse and not breathing, begin CPR immediately.
- Cover burns with clean dry dressings; keep victim warm and still.
- Do not delay CPR — rapid resuscitation greatly improves survival.
- Do not move victim unnecessarily if spinal injury is possible.
