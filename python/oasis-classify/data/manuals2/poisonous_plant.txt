Category: Poisonous Plant Exposure

- Skin contact (ivy, oak, sumac): wash with soap and water immediately — within 10 minutes greatly reduces reaction.
- Remove contaminated clothing without touching its outer surface.
- Apply cool compresses; use calamine lotion or hydrocortisone cream for itching.
- For plant or berry ingestion: call 911 and identify the plant if possible.
- Eye exposure: flush with clean water for 15-20 minutes without rubbing.
- Do not induce vomiting after plant ingestion without professional guidance.
- Do not burn poison ivy — inhaling the smoke causes severe airway injury.
