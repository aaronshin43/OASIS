Category: Opioid Overdose

- Call 911. Signs: unresponsive, slow/stopped breathing, blue lips, pinpoint pupils.
- Give naloxone (Narcan) -- nasal spray or injection; repeat if no response in 2-3 min.
- Rescue breathing: 1 breath every 5 sec until naloxone takes effect.
- When breathing returns, place in recovery position; wait for EMS.
- Do not leave the person alone -- overdose can return when naloxone wears off.
- Do not use cold shower or coffee -- these do not reverse overdose.
