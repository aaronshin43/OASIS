Category: Aggressive Outburst

- Prioritize your safety — leave and call 911 if physically threatened.
- Stay calm; keep voice low, slow, and even.
- Give physical space; position yourself near an exit.
- Use short, non-confrontational sentences: I can see you are very upset.
- Call 911 and move others away if aggression escalates to violence.
- Do not corner the person or block their exit.
- Do not shout, argue, or attempt physical restraint unless trained.
