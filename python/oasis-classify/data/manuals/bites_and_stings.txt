Category: Bites and Stings

STEPS:
1. Call 911 for anaphylaxis signs, known venomous bite, or multiple stings.
2. Snake bite: keep still, immobilize limb below heart level, remove jewelry, wash with soap and water.
3. Bee or wasp sting: scrape stinger sideways with a card, wash with water, apply ice.
4. Tick: grasp with fine tweezers close to skin, pull straight out steadily.
5. Animal or dog bite: wash wound with soap and water for 5 min; seek medical care.

- Do NOT cut or suck venom from a snake bite — ineffective and dangerous.
- Do NOT crush or squeeze a tick body while removing it.
