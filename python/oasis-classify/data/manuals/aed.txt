Category: AED / Defibrillator

STEPS:
1. Call 911 immediately if not already done.
2. Turn on the AED — press the power button or lift the lid.
3. Follow all voice prompts on the AED.
4. Attach pads to bare skin as shown on the pad diagram: one below the right collarbone, one below the left armpit.
5. When AED says "Stand clear" — ensure no one is touching the person, then press the shock button.
6. After shock, immediately resume CPR: 30 compressions then 2 rescue breaths.
7. Continue CPR and follow AED prompts until emergency services arrive.

- Do NOT touch the person when the AED is analyzing or delivering a shock.
- Do NOT use an AED on someone in water — move to a dry surface first.
